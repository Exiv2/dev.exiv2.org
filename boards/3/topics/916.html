Feature Request: make exiv2 work with partial image buffers/chunks

Added by Franz Buchinger over 10 years ago

Hi,

I want to offer a webservice that offers metadata extraction. The photo (jpeg or raw) should be uploaded in the browser and the response will be the exiv2's extracted metadata.

As the Exif/Iptc/Makernote metadata typically sits in the first 50 - 300 kilobytes of the file, my plan was to upload the image in 25 kilobyte chunks and create a partial image buffer from them on the server.
I wanted to run exiv2 on this buffer as soon a new chunk arrives. The upload should stop as soon as exiv2 is able to extract the desired metadata from the partial buffer.

However this doesn't seem to work (at least with pyexiv2 0.2.2/exiv2 0.19) - exiv2 insists on consuming the entire image buffer, although it isn't necessary.

Are there any chances this will change in the future?

regards,

Franz
