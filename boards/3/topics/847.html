RE: compiling exiv-0.21 on Solaris 10 with Sun Studio 12 - Added by Andreas Huggel over 10 years ago

It seems the Sun compiler doesn't like the template magic hidden behind the EXV_PRINT_* macros in this context, but I don't see why either. To confirm this, you could try to remove remove them here and you'll probably hit a similar problem elsewhere then.

A support channel for this compiler may be more helpful here, pls let us know if you find a solution.

        if (!lif) {
            return EXV_PRINT_TAG(canonCsLensType)(os, value, metadata);
        }

        else {
            EXV_PRINT_TAG_BITMASK(canonSiAFPointUsed)(os, value, pExifData);
        }

RE: compiling exiv-0.21 on Solaris 10 with Sun Studio 12 - Added by Ben Taylor over 10 years ago

looking back through our repository, we had exiv2-0.14 working with the
SunStudio12 compiler. The patches were done by Stefan Teleman of Sun
(and now Oracle) but no longer participates in the KDE4 Solaris project.
I think he had found the problem with the templates, and worked around it

The list of patches he created against 0.14 is at:
http://solaris.bionicmutton.org/hg/kde4-specs-460/file/26c2cab27bbe/specs/patches/exiv2/0.14

I will likely attempt a port forward of those patches for the project,
as I don't expect quick action from Oracle to fix any template problems.
