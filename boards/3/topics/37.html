pyexiv2 status

Added by Andreas Huggel almost 13 years ago

pyexiv2 is a Python binding to Exiv2. It's a project of its own, created by Olivier Tilloy, its homepage is here: http://tilloy.net/dev/pyexiv2/
Since I regularly get inquiries about pyexiv2 here and there hasn't been an update on its homepage for a while I asked Olivier about his plans.
The good news is that he is working on a new version of pyexiv2, even though currently with limited time. This is Olivier's reply:

"After a long while without any update on pyexiv2 I started to work on it
again about a month ago. pyexiv2 0.2 is currently in its very early
stages and clearly not usable yet, but compiles against exiv2 0.18 and
will fully support XMP metadata.
As the only time I can dedicate to this project is while commuting to
work in the morning (about 25min effective work time/day), the progress
is slow. I don't really want to set any deadlines, but it will
eventually be done and working. For the same lack of time reason, I
focus on development and there is a good chance the website and
documentation will remain outdated until pyexiv2 0.2 is out.
I will communicate my progress on the exiv2 ML as soon as I have a
decent first kind-of-working version [...]"

-ahu.
