RE: Tips/suggestions for exiv2 & CxImage usage? - Added by Robin Mills over 11 years ago

Hi Nikolai

That's an impressive library and it looks as though it has been maturing for much the same time as exiv2.

My initial thought is CxImage is for image data manipulation and exiv2 is for image meta-data manipulation. So they are complimentary (orthogonal).

Another thought I had was CxImage looks very windows-centric and exiv2 in platform neutral. My contribution to exiv2 has been to provide the DevStudio build environment to encourage the use of exiv2 on the Windows platform. Maybe I should investigate porting CxImage to Linux (and Mac) to encourage the adoption of CxImage on those platforms.

I have some questions for you:

1) Do you have any thought in mind that has prompted you to ask about this?
2) What are your thoughts about using exiv2 and CxImage together.
3) Have you been in touch with the CxImage team to ask them about including exiv2 into their demo application?

Thank you for bringing this to our attention.
