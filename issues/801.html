Note: mechanically the lenses are indeed different. The old (without "II") from ca. 2007 has a filter size of 72mm, the new (with "II") from 2011 has 62mm filter size. Zoom range is identical. The new one has improved glasses and from my experience seems to be slightly brighter.

By now I don't think that there is any lense related difference in the Exif data for both lenses (id 137).
But i'll try to have a closer look at this, since i have images of both versions.
