# Spend some time trying to figure out the answer to your question before asking for help. Read the available documentation, search this site, the "old mailing list":http://uk.groups.yahoo.com/group/exiv2 and use your favourite search engine to check if your issue has been raised before or your question has been answered already. Experiment with the software or the command line tool.





    4
    10
    Andreas Huggel
    
# Decide whether to post to the forum, raise a new bug or feature or add your comments to an existing forum post or issue. If you have found a bug and you're reasonably sure that it really is a new bug, it's best to open an issue for it. If the issue is with a specific image, we'll most likely need the image, please append it to the bug report if possible.





    5
    6
    Andreas Huggel
    
# In any case, posting here is better than writing to any project member in private as this allows others to discuss the issue and supply answers which a specific individual may not be able to give. Also, others can later find these answers without having to ask again and the whole community benefits.





    6
    9
    Andreas Huggel
    
# If you'd like to get involved in the development or contribute code, please post your ideas and intentions in the forum before sending a patch, unless it addresses a specific and straightforward open issue.
