Welcome to the Exiv2 community!¶

Exiv2 is the C++ library and command line utility to manage image metadata. This is the community website of the project. The Exiv2 homepage is http://www.exiv2.org/

From here you can access all project resources and interact with project members and Exiv2 users. Following a few simple guidelines ensures that you stand a good chance of getting an answer quickly. Your feedback is also a valuable contribution to the project and will help to improve the quality of the software and provide directions and encouragement for future development.
